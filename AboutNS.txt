NO SPOILERS- A TV Series managing app to make your binging 
easier

You can search for series, create and manage watchlists, 
and get timely updates on all new episodes of all your 
favourite series

You can search for any Series you like, and add them to
your watchlist using the "Add to watchlist" button.
You can view your watchlist at any time at 
Watchlist> Open watchlist from the menubar.

Use the "Save watchlist" button to save your watchlist
so it persists even if u exit the program and come back :)
